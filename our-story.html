Our Story

The journey of crafting essentials for modern living

The Beginning

TnsH was born from a simple yet profound idea: to create a brand that embodies minimalist elegance while delivering exceptional quality. Founded in 2020, our journey began with a small collection of carefully curated essentials designed to enhance everyday life.

We started with just three employees in a modest studio in Brooklyn, driven by our shared passion for clean design and sustainable practices. Our first collection sold out within days, affirming our belief that there was a demand for thoughtfully designed products that stood the test of time.

Our first studio in Brooklyn, 2020

"We don't follow trends. We create timeless designs that speak to the essence of modern living."

— Founder, TnsH
Our design process

Our Philosophy

At TnsH, we believe that less is more. Our design philosophy centers around creating products that are both functional and beautiful, with clean lines and thoughtful details that stand the test of time.

We are committed to responsible production methods, working closely with skilled artisans and ethical manufacturers to ensure that our products are made with care and consideration for both people and planet.

Our Values

Sustainability

We're committed to minimizing our environmental footprint through responsible sourcing and production.

Quality

We believe in creating products that are built to last, reducing waste and providing better value.

Community

We foster meaningful connections with our customers, partners, and the communities we serve.

Looking Forward

As we continue to grow, our commitment to our core values remains unwavering. We're excited about the future and the opportunity to bring our vision of mindful, modern living to more people around the world.

Thank you for being part of our journey. We invite you to explore our collections and join us in embracing the beauty of simplicity.
